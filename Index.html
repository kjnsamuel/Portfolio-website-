About BPIC

BPIC (Bi.P.C) stands for Biology, Physics, and Chemistry - the core subjects in this intermediate stream. This course is typically chosen by students aspiring for careers in:

  • Medicine (MBBS)
  • Pharmacy
  • Biotechnology
  • Biochemistry
  • Nursing
  • Agriculture Sciences

The BPIC curriculum provides strong foundation in life sciences and physical sciences, preparing students for various competitive exams like NEET, AIIMS, and other medical entrance examinations.
